About Us

Here at VidiSite, we are committed to highlighting the artistry of video games and collecting the best games, new and old, for everyone to enjoy. We came together as a group of friends and gamers who felt that the video game industry was tossed aside when considering art, literature, and acting, and turned that frustration into an idea: If you’re like us and wish the gaming industry got the credit it deserved, or you’re just looking for some new games to pick up, you’ll love our collections!
