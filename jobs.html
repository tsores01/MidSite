Careers

We’re currently looking to expand our business and that means we’re looking for new employees! Here are the positions we’re looking to fill:

Journalists (Full Time)

The core of our business is in researching, experiencing, then sharing games with our clients, so we’re always looking for more journalists to keep the flow of content coming. If you’re drawn to games and want to help others experience games the way you do, you’re the right kind of person for this position.

Editor (Full Time)

Our editors have been working tirelessly to keep the quality of our content top notch, but the workload has expanded, so we’re looking to hire another editor to work alongside our current ones. If you have a keen eye for detail and are comfortable working with deadlines, you’ll fit well in this position.

Website Developer/Designer (Part Time)

While we’re pretty happy with the state of our platform, we understand that there is always room for improvement in giving users the best experience possible. We’re looking for someone who has both technical design skills to help improve our website’s UI, functionality, and keep it modern.
